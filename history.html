Our History

The tale of how our company established

MARCH 2018

All of it started when Dr. Seri Intan Idayu is a lecturer in UiTM. She met with some students in the university that have the ability to talk and make people understand easily on what they are presenting to the class. It has been a dream for Dr. Seri Intan Idayu to have her own tuition that teach high-school students.

OCTOBER 2018

By then, Dr. Seri Intan has already form a tuition called Science Clinic + with recruiting 4 of her students that she saw potential in becoming a tutor for high-school students. The tuition is located near the UiTM and high-school. The tutor that was recruited by her are Izham Haiqal, Nor Azreen, Nabil Al-Hadif and Nursophelia Adira.

2019

Most of the classes held by these tutors have been successful with the attendance that reach to 40 people per class. The parents and students were also satisfy with the outcome of the teaching as most students get a good grade.

2020

The world then got hit by Covid Virus. The Malaysian government issued an order called Movement Control Order or known among malaysian as Perintah Kawalan Pergerakan (PKP). The business could not run at all. People are scared to get infected by the virus as it can lead to death.

2021

After endless online discussion and meeting, these 5 people has observed that by hosting an online tuition class can help in running back the business. There are 2 batch of high-school students that need to sit for SPM which is batch 2003 & 2004. We then started trial online tuition for 1 week to these students and it received a lot of supports.

2022 - 2024

After the strict order were lifted by the government, the Science Clinic+ run even better and less cost than before due to continuation in running the tuition by using online method instead of physical method. Science Clinic+ also received a lot of awards.
